Our Mission

Everyone could use a little more adventure in their life.


At Krespejo, we believe the best way to get this is through food. We're tired of going out to eat and always seeing the same few options in front of us, and this inspired us in our dreams to send Bostonians on a new food journey.


We want to start serving people something new, something tasty, and something to whet their appetites for new experiences.

Our Team

We are three undergrads from Northeastern University who share a passion for new foods, new business, and, of course, adventure. As members of the Husky Startup Challenge, we have been able to take our venture from ideation up through prototype creation. Our next steps are to expand upon our menu and purchase equipment in order to start serving the Northeastern community.

Our Menu

Our signature menu item is the Krespo. Tracing its origins back to hotteok, a traditional Korean street food, it achieves the food truck holy trinity: cheap, fast, and portable. Similar to a filled pancake, it's super versatile, allowing us to create many unique flavors and utilize concepts from around the world.
